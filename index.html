Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What is the maximum load capacity?

The maximum load capacity is 8kg per load. This ensures optimal washing and drying results, and guarantees that your clothes receive proper care and attention during the cleaning process.

Do you offer pickup and delivery?

Yes! We offer convenient pickup and delivery services. There may be minimal delivery fees depending on your location. Contact us at 09997644838 or message us on Facebook for delivery availability in your area.

Are detergent and fabric conditioner included?

Premium detergent is included in all services. Fabcon (fabric conditioner/softener) is included in our Full Service package. For Wash Only and Dry Only services, you can request additional fabcon for a small fee.

What are your operating hours?

We're open everyday from 7:00 AM to 7:00 PM. This includes weekdays, weekends, and holidays, so you can bring your laundry whenever it's convenient for you. Pickup and delivery services are also available during these hours.

How long does the full service take?

Our Full Service typically takes 24-48 hours depending on the volume. For urgent needs, we offer same-day service upon request. Please call us to confirm same-day availability.

Do you handle delicate fabrics?

Yes, we handle all types of fabrics with care, including delicate items. Our experienced staff uses appropriate washing and drying techniques for each fabric type. For special handling requests, please inform us when you drop off or book your laundry.
